Judge The Sentiment Of Content

Instructions

Overview

In this job, you will be presented with tweets and other online posts that are about Major League Baseball trade rumors. Review the tweets to determine the  sentiment so that we can have a greater understanding about the overall sentiment of the author.

Steps

  1. Read the tweet.
  2. Determine if the tweet is relevant to the topic.
  3. Click all links found in the text for additional context.
  4. Determine if the tweet is positive, neutral, or negative.

Rules & Tips

The posts can be classified as positive, negative or neutral: 

  • Positive means some aspects of the tweet uncover a positive mood, such as praise, recommendations or a favorable comparison.
  • Neutral means that the post is only informative in nature and provides no hint as to the mood of the author. 
  • Negative means some aspects of the tweet uncover a negative mood such as, criticism, insults or a negative comparison. 

Note:
Tweets that are purely factual (links to news articles without comment) are not necessarily Neutral â€“ consider whether the fact/news itself is Positive or Negative regarding the business and select one of those when possible.
